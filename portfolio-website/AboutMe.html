Pets & Pet Sitting

>for the curious, check out my HouseSitter.com profile here

Sabrina, my first dog I raised from a puppy, next to my baby nephew.

I've pet sat for over 15 years. I officially started by watching 7 rescue dogs and 2 cats simultaneously on a family friend's farm. I get the most enjoyment out of getting to know each animal's personality. They are all uniquely funny in their own way. Some are early risers that'll beat me to dawn while others will sleep in longer than me and have to be woken to go outside. Others are finicky eaters or happily entitled chunks that will eat another's food before they get the chance.

My first cat Boomer

Boomer was born a farm cat then raised by me to love dogs, human contact, and follow his instincts to fearlessly explore, hunt, or befriend animals smaller or bigger than him.

Here is Boomer indulging in a nap with my friend's Newfie-Lab mix.

One of my strongest motivators for continuing to pet sit is that I miss having my own pets and living somewhere they can be accomodated. I have long found a lot of enjoyment from visiting dog parks or taking dogs on a walk. In absence of pet ownership, I live vicariously by sitting for other's beloved pets.
